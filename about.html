About Me

I am an instructional designer in training with a strong interest in onboarding and process design. My work focuses on simplifying complex systems and helping people move from confusion to clarity.

Across different contexts, I have supported individuals by researching options, structuring information, and guiding decision-making. This includes helping people identify relevant job opportunities, understand processes, and take clearer next steps.

My experience includes designing structured learning programs, such as intensive language camps, where I aligned activities with clear learning outcomes while maintaining high levels of engagement.

I am particularly interested in designing onboarding experiences that reduce cognitive load, improve user confidence, and make processes easier to navigate.

I enjoy working at the intersection of structure and clarity — turning complex information into something usable, actionable, and easy to understand.
